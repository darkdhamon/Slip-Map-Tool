Known limits of Star Generator

- data size: even if I reduced it, it' s still high. For a sector of 27000 parsecs cube with a density of 8%,
the different files take about 3.5 Mo. So if you choose a sector with a size of 90 beware of the disk
space and CPU-time used.

- maximum size of sectors:
star systems 2147483647
planets 2147483647
moons 2147483647
alien races 65535

Don' t try to exceed these limits, or you could have some problems when browsing the sector.

If you find any bugs, please email me at aina@geocities.com

Hardware & software recommendations

It should work on any 486+ PC under Windows 95/98/2000 and NT4.
In order to use Star Browser your graphic card should support a 800x600 display.
With an AMD K-2 300 it will take 150 seconds to create a 25 size sector with a density
of 8%.

All these programs were written in Delphi 3.

Units

AU: Astronomical Unit = 149 597 870 km (represents the distance between Sol and Earth)

Pc : Parsec = 3.26 Light Years = 63 241 AU

Star mass and luminosity are based on Sol values. A star with a luminosity of 2 is two times
brighter than Sol.

Luminosity= 10^(4.7-Mv)/2.5 with Mv= the star absolute magnitude

Magnetic field: Earth value= 0.3 to 0.6 gauss

Albedo: represents the astral body reflective power. (Ice has an albedo of 0.9)
